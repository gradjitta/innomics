Perl5 for biology tutorial

Practical Extraction and Reporting Language.

Welcome to perl for biology tutorial.This tutorial is aimed at the biologists who were never been to programming Or who were experienced in languages like c/c++,but have no idea of perl.I tried to explain as lucid as possible although I cannot make everything look simple.Remember!! This tutorial covers most of the things that a biologist need to know about perl,but I cannot guarantee you that it covers everything in perl.Any programming language can be mastered through practice only.I have included practice questions at the end of each chapter,I expect that you will finish all those programs before proceeding to next chapter.

All the examples in this tutorial are tested with perl 5.8.8 under WINDOWS platform...If there are any queries regarding this tutorial...contact me at nvteja@gmail.com
